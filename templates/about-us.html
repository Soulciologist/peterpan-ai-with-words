About Us

Peter Pan was originally made by us three high-school students in Vancouver, Canada: Daniel H. Liu, Chloe Nguyen, and Prajwal Prashanth.

We worked together to make the initial concepts and prototype of Peter Pan. At first, we envisioned an app that could make longevity and a healthy life accessible to all people. We realized that maintaining a healthy life is a difficult goal with other responsibilities and time-consuming tasks. Longevity wasn't a normal consideration for most, since we all have other things to do besides concentrate on our health.

We thought about parents as an especially clear case. Parents have to balance their children's life, their own life, and all their responsibilities and simple enjoyment. In all this chaos and splintered work, parents just can't ponder their glucose all day. That's where Peter Pan comes in: we wanted to make a service that manages your glucose curves and gives actionable feedback to help you maintain your health, your energy, and extend your healthspan and lifespan.
