About Peter Pan

Peter Pan is an AI prototype that has one goal: help people frictionlessly manage their blood glucose levels through multiple means, making long healthspan and high energy accessible for everyone.

You might be asking: "Why blood glucose? What does sugar have to do with my health, precisely?" It's simple: many negative effects on health (from small problems like fat gain or energy crashs to diseases like diabetes) stem from energy mismanagement.

It all has to do with glucose curves. When you eat food, the carbohydrates are coverted into glucose, which is sent through your blood. Over time, this glucose is consumed by your cells using insulin and the curve drops. However, when the glucose curve is above a certain amount, your body can't use it all and instead converts it into stored fat. These high points of glucose result in "spikes" that eventually crash to low glucose, which you experience as an energy crash and feeling awful.

Volatile, "spiky" glucose curves can cause people to develop diseases over time, such as diabetes mellitus. Arguably worse, these spikes also cause advanced glycation end-products (AGEs) to form in your blood. AGEs stuff up the bloodstream and build plaque, which eventually becomes cardiovascular disease and heart difficulties.

As such, for healthy, energetic day-to-day life and a long, healthy lifespan, Peter Pan is here to help!

About Us

Peter Pan was originally made by us three high-school students in Vancouver, Canada: Daniel H. Liu , Chloe Nguyen , and Prajwal Prashanth .

We worked together to make the initial concepts and prototype of Peter Pan, as well as defending it as a Moonshot project. At first, we envisioned an app that could make longevity and a healthy life accessible to all people. We realized that maintaining a healthy life is a difficult goal with other responsibilities and time-consuming tasks. Longevity wasn't a normal consideration for most, since we all have other things to do besides concentrate on our health.

We thought about parents as an especially clear case. Parents have to balance their children's life, their own life, and all their responsibilities and simple enjoyment. In all this chaos and splintered work, parents just can't ponder their glucose all day. That's where Peter Pan comes in: we wanted to make a service that manages your glucose curves and gives actionable feedback to help you maintain your health, your energy, and extend your healthspan and lifespan.
